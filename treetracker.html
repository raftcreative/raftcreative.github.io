How It Works

Buy Lip Balm

Every lip balm you buy gives us the ability to plant one tree in an area that desperately needs it. See how many trees you have helped us plant on our treetracker.

Plant Trees

We work with our partners around the world to plant trees where they're needed most. Like areas affected by profit driven deforestion, wildfires, and climate change.

Remove Carbon

Each tree you help us plant will remove 48 pounds of carbon dioxide from the atmostphere every year. The more lip balm you buy, the more trees we plant and the bigger impact we make together.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why trees?
    We care about both people and the environment. Our grass roots model, where we work with local communities around the world, brings more benefit to the people of these exploited lands - and to the environment we all share - than focusing on either individually.
  • How do you pick where to plant trees?
    We work with our partners to both assist people in developing communities that come to us for help, and to identify specific projects in need of support.
  • Why don't you just plant trees and not sell lip balm?
    Honestly we wish we could, but that simply isn't sustainable. We need a source of income to support the costs of planting trees and hiring locals in developing communities.
  • Can you provide proof of the trees you've planted?
    You can contact any of our partners and verify with them that our contributions, both financial and in person, have been made.
  • How can I track how many trees have been planted?
    The treetracker shows a current count of how many trees we've planted and where. Our partners ocassionally send us progress, plus before and after, photos of each of the planting sites where we work. These photos will be added to our treetracker.
